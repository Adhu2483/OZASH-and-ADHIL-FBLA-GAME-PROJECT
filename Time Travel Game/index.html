⏳ Time Traveler: Apollo Mission

Level 1 — Meteor Catcher: Collect 10 meteors within 30 seconds!

Level 2 — Lunar Rover: Dodge asteroids for 15 seconds!

Level 3 — Space Quiz: Answer questions on space history to earn points!

Controls: Arrow Keys or WASD to move. Use number keys (1/2/3) to answer quiz questions.

Win / Loss Conditions

  • Level 1 (Meteor Catcher): Collect 10 meteors in 30 seconds to win.
  • Level 2 (Lunar Rover): Dodge all obstacles for 15 seconds to win. Hitting any obstacle = lose.
  • Level 3 (Space Quiz): Answer 5 randomized questions. Get at least 3 correct to win.
