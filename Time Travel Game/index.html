⏳ Time Traveler: Apollo Mission

Level 1 — Fuel Catcher: Collect 10 fuels within 30 seconds!

Level 2 — Ninja Rover: Dodge asteroids for 15 seconds!

Level 3 — Space Quiz: Answer questions on space history to earn points!

Controls: Arrow Keys or W and A to move. Use number keys (1/2/3) to answer quiz questions.

Win / Loss Conditions

  • Level 1 (Fuel Catcher): Collect 10 Fuels in 30 seconds to win. Fail to collect all 10 Fuels within 30 seconds = lose.
  • Level 2 (Ninja Rover): Dodge all asteroids for 15 seconds to win. Hitting any asteroid = lose.
  • Level 3 (Space Quiz): Answer 5 randomized questions. Get at least 3 correct to win. Fail to get 3 correct = lose.
